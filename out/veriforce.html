  • If Contact opens Step 1 email AND clicks renewal portal CTA within 48 hours but does not complete renewal within 72 hours → Acceleration path: suppress Step 2 banner default timing; immediately surface in-app banner on next login session and simultaneously create low-priority AE awareness task — do not wait for Day 7 threshold

  • If Account.churn_risk_score crosses threshold ≥ 85 at any point (warehouse real-time update) → Escalate account to AE priority queue: create Salesforce Task with Priority = Critical and ActivityDate = today; pull account out of automated sequence; flag for manual AE outreach within 4 business hours; notify AE via Slack webhook

Pipeline projection
Expected renewals
~101 account renewals (12% of 840 accounts); estimated ARR recovery of $1.2M–$1.6M assuming average Tier-2 ARR of ~$14,300/account; AE-assisted renewals projected at 30–35% of total conversions (30–35 accounts via Steps 3 & 5)
AE efficiency
AE task volume capped at ~168 tasks (Step 3 triggers on ~20% of 840 accounts showing engagement signals); Step 5 LinkedIn outreach limited to churn_risk ≥ 70 non-responders (~200–250 contacts); total AE labor estimated at 140–180 hours across the quarter, compared to ~520 hours for fully manual outreach — efficiency gain of ~65%
Runtime
21-day active sequence per contact cohort; full campaign closes Day 28 (Day 21 final touch + 7-day reply/conversion window); reporting cadence: Day 7 (early signal review), Day 14 (variant selection), Day 21 (final push), Day 28 (pipeline close and retrospective)
Downside scenario
Downside scenario (probability ~25%): email deliverability degradation or compliance data sync lag reduces segment accuracy by 20–30%, limiting addressable pool to ~600 accounts. Engagement rates fall to Step 1 open rate ~18%, portal CTA click rate ~3.5%. Renewal conversion drops to 5–6% of addressable pool (~36 accounts), recovering ~$515K ARR. AE task volume remains constant but conversion from AE-assisted path also drops proportionally. Mitigation levers: (1) warm up sending domain 7 days pre-launch; (2) run warehouse-to-Salesforce compliance status reconciliation within 48 hours of campaign kick-off; (3) activate SMS channel as parallel Step 1 fallback for contacts with verified mobile numbers if email open rates fall below 15% at Day 3 check.
