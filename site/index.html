Frequently asked questions

What counts as a "qualified meeting"?

A booked call with a decision-maker or technical lead at a company matching your ICP, who showed an active buying signal in the last 30 days. If they no-show, you don't pay.

How long until I see meetings?

Most customers see their first meeting within 2-3 weeks. Signal detection starts within 24 hours of ICP configuration. Outreach begins as signals are detected and contacts are enriched.

What are the 6 signal sources?

GitHub (new repos, dependency adoption, contributor activity), ArXiv (research papers by corporate authors), HuggingFace (model uploads), job postings (hiring for roles in your domain), funding rounds (fresh capital for relevant companies), and LinkedIn (content engagement, role changes).

How is this different from Apollo/ZoomInfo/Clay?

Those tools give you a database to search. We give you meetings. We handle signal detection, enrichment, outreach copywriting, email sending, and booking — end to end. You just show up to the call.

What if my ICP is very niche?

Signal-based prospecting works best for niche ICPs. The more specific your target, the higher the signal quality. We configure custom scanning rules for your exact domain — that's the 15-minute setup.

Is there a minimum commitment?

No. No setup fees, no monthly minimums, no long-term contracts. You pay per meeting. If it doesn't work, you've spent nothing.

Can I see the open-source toolkit?

Yes. The scanning, scoring, and stacking engine is open source with 448 tests and 91% coverage. The service layer (outreach sequencing, enrichment, booking) runs on top of it.
